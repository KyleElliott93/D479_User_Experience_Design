How To Get Here

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.

Once You Get Here

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing. Taxis are available in the city and bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law).

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. everyday. Private buses serve the rest of the island. Get directions to the nearest bus stop.

Rental cars are available at the airport.
