Sight Seeing

Most people visiting Taniti enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. The Taniti Government has several maintained hiking trails with easy to follow directions to the trailheads.

Entertainment in Merriton Landing

There are other things to do in Taniti besides the beach, rainforest, and volcano. There is guided fishing tours, snorkeling, zip-lining in the rainforest, visiting several pubs, dancing at a new dance club, taking helicopter rides of the island, playing golf, and a bowling alley.
